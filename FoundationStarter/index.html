Navient Fails to Improve



By Ian Dalrymple

Since 2020, the year that Navient filed a motion for their lawsuit from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau to be dismissed, things haven’t improved at the company, in fact, they’ve gotten worse.

In 2020 Navient had 44 student loan complaints coming from Maryland, that number dipped to 33 in 2021, but in 2022 the number of complaints jumped to 51. Similarly in Virginia, a neighboring state with approximately 2 million more people, complaints have only grown since 2020.

Complaints in the two states peaked in August last year as students returned to school, and they bottomed out in November.
